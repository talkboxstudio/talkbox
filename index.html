Services We Provide

Take a look at how we can transform the sound of your podcast.

Detailed Editing

Each episode you produce is going to have its own unique quirks that need to be ironed out. We listen to each recording thoroughly and edit accordingly.

Audio Mastery

We balance your audio to make sure it's not too loud or too soft.
All audio is leveled near -16 LUFS, the standard volume level for podcast audio.

Noise Reduction

High-pitched hissing or abstract background noise can be easily reduced. We cut these distractions out to create the most seamless sound possible.

File Backups

All of your episodes will be backed up on our servers for posterity.
Your files will also be organized & delivered to you via the Google Drive cloud.

Quick Turnaround

We ensure a timely editing turnaround, meaning we will always consider your podcast's publishing schedule and get each edit to you on time; even in a last-minute pinch.

Technical Support

We can walk you through the proccess of publishing your podcasts to all major streaming platforms, or answer any questions you may have about producing a podcast.
